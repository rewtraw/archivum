Archivum

Your personal library, infinitely organized

A native macOS app that acquires, archives, and enriches your digital text collection with AI-powered understanding.

Download for macOS View Source

Capabilities

Everything your library needs

From import to research, every step is enriched by AI and built to run locally.

AI-Powered Import

Drop any document and Claude or Ollama extracts metadata, structure, and full text automatically. Titles, authors, tags, and descriptions appear like magic.

Agentic RAG Chat

A multi-step research assistant that searches, reads summaries, refines queries, and synthesizes answers across your entire library with citations.

Smart Search

Full-text keyword search and semantic vector search work together. Find passages by meaning, not just exact words, across every document you own.

Hierarchical Summaries

Section-level, document-level, and library-wide AI summaries give you a bird's-eye view. The agent uses these as a navigation map before diving into details.

Local-First

SQLite database, local embeddings, and optional local LLMs via Ollama. Your documents never leave your Mac unless you want them to.

Every Format

PDF, EPUB, MOBI, DjVu, HTML, Markdown, plain text, comic archives, and audio/video with Whisper transcription. If it has words, Archivum reads it.

Workflow

Three steps to a living library

I

Import

Drop files or paste URLs. Archivum deduplicates, stores originals by content hash, and extracts text locally.

II

Enrich

AI extracts metadata, generates summaries, creates embeddings, and builds a searchable index of every passage.

III

Research

Ask questions. The agentic assistant searches, cross-references, and synthesizes answers from across your collection.
